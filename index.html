ACL Workshop on NLP in E-Commerce

Overview

Natural Language Processing (NLP) plays an irreplaceable role in modern digital shopping applications. At the same time, the E-Commerce settings present exciting novel opportunities and challenges to NLP tasks. However, the domain of E-Commerce NLP receives little attention in the academic literature. The goal of this workshop is to bridge the gap between researchers and practitioners to study the unique set of problems in the intersection of NLP and E-Commerce. The E-Commerce workshop has been hosted at SIGIR since 2017, and has received great responses. We expect to at least replicate the same level of success in the ACL community.
